FROM HUMBLE BEGINNINGS TO CULINARY LEGACY

Founded in 1994 by a close-knit group of Japanese friends, RE&S was born from one simple yet powerful dream: to bring the warmth, flavors, and rich culinary traditions of Japan to Singapore. What started as a humble vision quickly grew into a journey of dedication, creativity, and a deep love for authentic Japanese cuisine.

Today, every dish we serve is crafted with the same passion and attention to detail that inspired our founders decades ago.
