FROM HUMBLE BEGINNINGS TO CULINARY LEGACY

Founded in 1994 by a group of Japanese friends, RE&S began with one simple dream: to share the warmth, flavours, and culture of Japan with Singapore. What started as a small eatery soon grew into a collection of restaurants known for authentic taste, heartfelt service, and innovative creations.

Guided by the belief that food brings people together, RE&S has expanded across the island, staying committed to quality and the spirit of Japanese hospitality. Today, every dish we serve carries the same passion that started it all.
